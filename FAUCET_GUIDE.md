## Troubleshooting

**"Faucet is empty" or "Rate limited"**
- Try again in a few hours
- Try a different faucet from the options above
- Join Discord and ask in #testnet-support

**"Transaction failed"**
- Make sure you're on the correct network (ZetaChain Athens Testnet)
- Clear your browser cache and try again
